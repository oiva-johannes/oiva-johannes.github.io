I'm technologist who's interested in a variety of things. I have strong conviction that deep technological and scientific progress will lead us to a better future. My overall philosophy for life is to get things done and to keep moving forward.

My strengths are primarily in mathematics and computer science, but I also like to think about societies and societal change. I'm starting my second year as a university student majoring in mathematics. I also enjoy cycling, reading hard SF and making great pizzas. There is something weirdly technical and mechanical in the process of making pizza dough and the base. It's like an art and a skill at the same time; it reminds me a lot about programming.
